Content

Home page

The main goal of this website is to attract clients who are seeking professional cleaning and organizing services for either their residence or business. Whether clients need a one-time deep cleaning, regular maintenance, or help decluttering their space, this website will serve to inform them about the services offered and allow them to easily book consultations. Clients will be looking for more information regarding the quality of services provided and for reassurance that their home will be treated professionally and with care. Clients will be able to quickly access all the information they need, helping them make an informed decision. A critical aspect of the website's design is user experience . It is imperative that the website is relaxing, visually pleasing, and easy to navigate to create a positive first impression. I used calming colors such as lavender and other complimentary colors to give off the sense of relaxation, because cleaning is all about that feeling of comfort while sitting in your spotless, organized space. My design focuses on a calm and quality experience without distractions. I am going to ensure that all aspects of my website are easy to find and even easier to use. I hope that this encourages the users to engage with my website and the services offered.

Images for the Home page

Services

This page of the website will include all the photos from previously cleaned homes and give an idea of what to expect from this business. One of the main factors that will contribute to attracting potential clients to this website is reference photos. Customers who are considering our services will want to see photos showcasing the exact skills this business will provide and by using before and after photos, we will be ensuring that the potential clients see the quality of our work. Hiring a cleaning company is a commitment and these photos will help customers make that difficult decision to hire a cleaning service. The specific services rendered include deep-cleaning, move out cleans, standard cleans, organizing and much more upon request. Additionally, our cleaners are adaptable, and we pride ourselves on our ability to meet the unique needs of each customer. Whether clients need help organizing a home office, decluttering a garage, or prepping a home for sale, we are equipped to handle a wide variety of tasks. Customers will also be able to book consultations and get a hold of the owners straight from the website.

Images for the Page 2

Contact Us

In addition to reference photos, this website will provide clear and accessible pricing information. Nobody wants to hire a service if they do not know what the overall cost will be and that is why I will ensure that all pricing details are readily available on this page of the website. Clients will not have to go digging either to find pricing because I will have it clearly labeled and organized, minimizing any confusion of frustration. Pricing will be broken down by service type, giving the customers full transparency before a consultation is booked. Before a client even books an appointment, they will have access to accurate pricing information and be fully aware of the services rendered so there are no surprises in cost down the line. This page will have all of my contact information to allow for customers to book a consultation. Monday through Friday, 8am until 4pm will be the hours clientele can expect to have their houses cleaned and this portion of the website will clearly outline that.
