Cleaning Services

Looking for inspiration? Look no further! We offer a diverse collection of stunning photos that will not only impress but also inspire you to trust us with your cleaning needs. We offer a wide range of cleaning services to suit your needs, including standard cleans, deep cleans, move-out cleans, and more upon request. Our standard clean includes light dusting, vacuuming, wiping down surfaces, and tidying up kitchens, bedrooms, and bathrooms. Move-out cleans encompass everything in the standard clean, with the added touch of wiping down baseboards, walls, cabinets, cleaning out spaces, refrigerators, and any other storage areas. Move-out cleans are priced at $50 per hour, per cleaner. Additional services beyond the standard cleaning are considered deep cleaning and are charged at an extra $10 per hour.
